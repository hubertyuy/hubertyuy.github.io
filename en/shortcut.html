Placing app shortcuts on the home screen

Users can arrange shortcuts to their preferred apps on the home screen.

1Select the home screen.

The app menu screen will appear.

2Press and hold the app you would like to create a shortcut for, drag that app’s icon onto the home screen, and release it.

Place widgets on the home screen

Widgets are apps which can operate directly on the home screen.
They can be used to perform searches or other simple operations.

1Select on the home screen. 。

The app menu will display on the screen.

2Select “Widgets”.

The widget's menu will appear on the screen.

3Press and hold the widget you would like to place, drag that widget to your preferred location on the home screen, and release.

Deleting app shortcuts and widgets from the home screen

Press and hold an app shortcut or widget, then drag it to the × area, and release.
