Taking photos

The device is equipped with a 500 megapixel frontal camera and an 800 megapixel high resolution main camera.

1 Select “camera” from the app menu.

2If the device is in a mode other than “camera”, first swipe from left to right and select

3Select the “camera” button to shoot a photo.

The camera focuses automatically, but can also be manually focused, and the focal point and exposure adjusted by touching the screen.

Use your fingers to zoom the camera in and out.

To view previously taken photos or videos, use your fingers to scroll on the left side of the screen, or browse pictures using the “photo gallery” app.

Select in the upper right corner of the screen to display the Details Menu.
