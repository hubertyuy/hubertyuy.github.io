Charging the battery

Please connect this device to a computer or power transformer using a USB Type-C charging cable.

  • This product’s built-in battery is not fully charged at the time of purchase.
    Please charge the battery before using the product.
  • A power adapter is not included with this product. Please purchase a power adapter separately.
    Charging cable specifications: AC-USB,Input:100-240V,Output:5V/2.4A,12V/1.5A (quick charger)
  • While the device closed and the battery is charged below 15%, the LED indicator will illuminate red light.
  • While the device closed and the battery is charged between 15% ~ 90%, the LED indicator will illuminate orange light.
  • While the device closed and the battery is charged above 90%, the LED indicator will illuminate green light.
  • The product can be fully charged in about 2 hours by using a power adapter.
    Charging the product with a USB cable will take longer.
  • Please turn off the LCD display while charging the device, or the device may not fully charge or charge more slowly.
