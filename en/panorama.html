Shooting panoramas

In panorama mode, slowly move the screen from left to right or up to down to take a 180 degree panoramic photo.

Note

The front lens cannot be used to shoot a panorama.

1 Select the “camera” icon from the app menu.

2If the camera is in a mode other than panorama, swipe from left to right and select

Touch the panorama icon to select a panorama type：

Types
Fisheye mode
Wide-angle mode
Vertical mode
Horizontal mode


　

3Select the “take picture” button to begin shooting.

4When gray dots appear on the screen, slowly turn the cell phone while staying on each target, until the gray dots turn blue.

5Continue shooting until all of the gray dots have turned blue. Once you have finished shooting, touch the checkmark on the edge of the screen.

Note

If the camera’s direction or speed changes too quickly, the screen will notify you that you are moving too quickly. Please move the camera slowly.
