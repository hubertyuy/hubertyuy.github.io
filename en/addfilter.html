  • Editing Photos

This device is equipped with basic image cropping, framing, and color editing functions.

Photos can be edited through the app’s “photos”.

Tap the photo you want to edit

Tap Edit. At the bottom, choose what to do:

  Tune

1Automatically adjust color and exposure, manually change lighting, manually adjust color, or add effects.




2An example of vignette:

While you're editing, you can touch and hold the photo to compare your edits to the original.

3At the bottom right, tap Done.

4At the top right, tap SAVE.
An edited version of the image will be saved separately from the original image.

  Filter

1Add or adjust a filter.




2An example of Eris:

While you're editing, you can touch and hold the photo to compare your edits to the original.

3At the bottom right, tap Done.

4At the top right, tap SAVE.
An edited version of the image will be saved separately from the original image.

  Crop

1Crop or rotate.

3At the bottom right, tap Done.

4At the top right, tap SAVE.
