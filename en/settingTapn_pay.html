Setting Tap & pay

Currently there are thousands of merchants who accept contactless payments.
You can use tap & pay with your KALOS 2 when making purchases from these merchants.

Set up this device’s tap & pay function

You must enable Near Field Communication (NFC) before using tap & pay

Setting up which apps use tap & pay

If you use multiple apps that support tap & pay, you can select which one to use by default when using tap & pay at a store.

1First, open the payment app and confirm that tap & pay is enabled.

2Open settings, scroll down to tap & pay, and select a default app.

Turn off tap & pay

Disable NFC to turn off tap & pay.

Using tap & pay

When making a payment at a terminal, place the back of the KALOS2 near any terminal which can accept contactless payment.
